Standard Operational Guide

Dolu Logistics v1.2

This is the standard day-to-day guide for using the Dolu Logistics v1.2 platform. For a deeper technical and system-wide breakdown, please refer to the Master System Overview document.

1. Customer Workflow

Service Experience
  • Step 1: Request Pickup: Enter Pickup and Drop-off locations. The system instantly draws the route and calculates the price based on PH Zones.
  • Step 2: Booking Details: Provide the names and phone numbers for both the Sender and the Receiver. Ensure addresses are accurate for the rider.
  • Step 3: Payment & Confirmation: Follow the on-screen payment instructions. Once completed, a unique Tracking ID is generated.
  • Step 4: Tracking: Use the Tracking ID to monitor the parcel's progress on the interactive map and see real-time status updates.

2. Admin Workflow

Operations
  • Confirming Orders: Once a customer pays, the Bell Notification will ring. Review the order and tap "Confirm."
  • Dispatching: Go to the Dispatch Desk, select an available rider, and set the status to "In Progress."
  • Reporting: Use the "Export to Excel" tool in the Bookings tab for your daily revenue audit.

3. Rider Workflow

Field Operations
  • Accepting Jobs: Check your dashboard for the NEW LOAD badge. Tapping a job shows you the full pickup/dropoff route.
  • Contacting: Use the one-tap WhatsApp or Call buttons to coordinate with the sender or receiver.
  • Confirming Delivery: Use the "Slide to Deliver" handle at the destination to safely close the job.

4. Standard Operational Workflow

Example Logistics Cycle: Urban to Regional Delivery

  1. Initial Booking: A customer creates a request from Old GRA to Airport Road. High-Zone pricing is applied automatically.
  2. Confirmation: The Admin uses the Notification Center to confirm the booking status upon verification of payment.
  3. Assignment: The Dispatcher assigns a rider via the Dispatch Desk. The status is moved to In Progress.
  4. Transit Phase: The assigned rider receives an automated notification. They utilize the integrated contact tools to notify the sender and receiver.
  5. Confirmation of Handover: Upon arrival, the rider utilizes the Tactile Swipe Handle to confirm physical delivery and handover.
  6. Completion: The system automatically records the revenue and updates the public tracking status to Delivered.
